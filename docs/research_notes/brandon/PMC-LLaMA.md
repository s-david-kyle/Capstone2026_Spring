# Overview
PMC-LLaMA [31] is specifically designed for medical applications, incorporating biomedical and clinical data through a two-step training process: data-centric knowledge injection fol- lowed by medical-specific instruction tuning. This model is based on LLaMA-v2 with 13B parameters.
