# Overview
The dataset contains patients synthesized using a proprietary medical knowledge base and a commercial rule-based ASD system.
- Patients in the dataset are characterized by their socio-demographic data, a pathology they are suffering from, a set of symptoms and antecedents related to this pathology, and a differential diagnosis.
- The symptoms and antecedents can be binary, categorical and multi-choice, with the potential of leading to more efficient and natural interactions between ASD/AD systems and patients.
- To the best of their knowledge, this is the first <mark>large-scale dataset that includes the differential diagnosis, and non-binary symptoms and antecedents.</mark>
