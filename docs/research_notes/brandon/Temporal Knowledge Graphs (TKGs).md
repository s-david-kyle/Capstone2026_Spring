A special subset of [[Dynamic Knowledge Graphs (DKGs)]]
- They have the added capability of storing event timestamps
- They ensure data consistency with regards to time
- Research on TKGs focuses on studying individual nodes and edges
	- Useful for link prediction and time prediction that aim to anticipate events between entities and the time of their occurrences
	- Note: DKGs and TKGs are not mutually exclusive, with work that mixes tasks that both can do
