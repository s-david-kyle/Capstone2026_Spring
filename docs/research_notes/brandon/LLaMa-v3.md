Local LLM that is fine-tuned for differential diagnosis, to enhance diagnostic accuracy and reliability.
- Uses Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) for fine-tuning, to enable high task-specific performance and meet stringent computational and privacy requirements
- Compared to LLaMA-v2, which is used by PMC-LLaMA and Me-LLaMA, <mark>LLaMA-v3 demonstrates enhanced capability in understanding complex language patterns, which is essential for processing nuanced medical information and performing differential diagnosis</mark>.
-  Utilized the <mark>“Meta-Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct”</mark> variant
